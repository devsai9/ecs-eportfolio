Summary of Assignment & Requirements:

Based upon the computer buying interview your task is to give the person you interviewed at least 4 options on which computers to buy.

Challenges & My Solution:

The assignments wanted me to research 4 computers that met my interview notes. My interview notes wanted me to find a computer with specifications that almost never seen together in one laptop. And if a laptop had most of the specifications, it was way past the price point (another requirement). So I decided to find laptops that met as many specifications as possible, even if they went over the price point.
