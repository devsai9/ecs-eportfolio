Summary of Assignment & Requirements:

Pick your favorite Artificial Intelligence (AI) from a book, comic book, and/or movie. Create a presentation, write a paper, or create a video that answers the following about your fictional AI. All references must be cited using either MLA or APA citation.

  1. What fictional AI did you pick? Explain the AI characters purpose in the story. (Minimum of three sentences)
  2. Describe the physical attributes of your AI. Provide video or images from the story.
  3. Can your AI learn and/or develop new concepts. Provide examples and explain from the story.
  4. What is the definition of artificial intelligence? (Minimum of one sentence)
  5. Based upon your definition of artificial intelligence (AI), how did the fictional AI fit the description of AI. Please provide examples. (Minimum of 4 sentences)
  6. Name three or more ethical issues that were addressed about the fictional AI? (Minimum of 2 sentences for each issue)
  7. What is acoustic processing and can your fictional AI perform this? Based upon your definition why or why not?
  8. What is speech recognition and can your fictional AI perform this? Based upon your definition why or why not?
  9. What is facial recognition and could your fictional AI perform this? Based upon your definition why or why not?
  10. Did this or could this AI cause the Rise of the Machines (refer to the article in the module). What controls should of been implemented to not allow this to occur? If it did not what controls could be put in place to prevent this?

Challenges & My Solution:

The assignments wanted me to pick an AI from a book, comic book, or movie. However, I have never read/watched anything that had any kind of AI in it. So I decided to use ChatGPT as my AI of choice as it was free to use and I already knew a lot about it.
